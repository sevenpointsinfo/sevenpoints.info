About Seven Points

Building trust through excellence and professional execution

Seven Points is a premier construction and event management company based in Duhok, Kurdistan Region. With over 5 years of proven experience, we have successfully delivered 150+ projects across commercial, corporate, and government sectors.

Our commitment to precision, professionalism, and punctuality has made us a trusted partner for organizations seeking reliable solutions. We combine innovative approaches with rigorous quality control to ensure every project exceeds expectations.

From large-scale construction projects to sophisticated event productions, our dedicated team brings expertise, discipline, and unwavering commitment to excellence in every engagement.
